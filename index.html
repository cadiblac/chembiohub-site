Need to record, index and search compounds?

ChemReg can help.

Have assay data to manage? Interested in publically available standards?

AssayReg can help.

Need a simple tool to keep track of chemicals in your lab?

InventoryReg can help.

These tools all form part of the ChemBio Hub Platform

What we've achieved

ChemBio Hub has run 2 symposia so far for academia and industry, with another symposium in the works - details here

ChemBio Hub has had representatives giving presentations at EuroSciPy 2015 and RDKit UGM 2015
