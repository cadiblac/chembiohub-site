Alternatively you can request a demonstration from a member of the ChemBio Hub team.

If you are a developer, you can pull down the code to customise and run your own instance of ChemReg. The code is Open Source and available on Github.

Contact us with your thoughts

At ChemBio Hub we want this project to be useful to the people who are using it, not just a polished piece of code. We welcome any feedback you may have - whether that is ideas, feature requests, or problems that you have encountered when using the system. AssayReg is for the chemical biology community and beyond. We also want to hear from developers who work within the areas of chemical biology with their thoughts on the tool.

You can contact us about AssayReg either by email or tweet @OxChemBioHub and use the hashtag #assayreg.
